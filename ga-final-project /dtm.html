DEEP TISSUE MASSAGE SERVICES

DEEP TISSUE MASSAGE SERVICE

Variations without the complications

This massage service focuses on the deeper layers of muscle tissue. It aims to release the chronic patterns of tension in the body through deep finger pressure and slow strokes on the contracted areas, either following or going across the fiber’s of the muscles, tendons and fascia, to “unstick” the muscle fibers while releasing deeply-held patterns of tension, while relaxing and soothing the muscle. It is both corrective and therapeutic.

How Can Deep Tissue Massage Services Help In Rehabilitation

Massage has a number of important benefits for clients with any type of injury. By working to break up adhesions, deep tissue massage can help patients recover lost movement and flexibility while increasing circulation for better healing.Massage services alleviate muscle pain while relieving adhesions. Adhesions are bands of rigid, scar tissue that can connect tissues that were never normally meant to be connected, thereby causing restricted movement, pain, and blocked circulation.

Benefits

Eliminate

Massages help to break up and eliminate scar tissue which may cause some soreness; however, if the massage is done correctly you should feel better than ever within a day or two.

Increase

Massage services alleviate muscle pain while relieving adhesions , it can help patients recover lost movement and flexibility while increasing circulation.

Release

Massage service is used to release chronic muscle tension through slower strokes and more direct deep pressure or friction applied across the grain of the muscles not with the grain.
