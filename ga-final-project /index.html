OUR SERVICES

DEEP TISSUE MASSAGE SERVICE

Deep tissue massage service focuses on the deeper layers of muscle tissue. It aims to release the chronic patterns of tension in the body through deep finger pressure and slow strokes on the contracted areas, either following or going across the fiber’s of the muscles, tendons and fascia, to “unstick” the muscle fibers while releasing deeply-held patterns of tension, while relaxing and soothing the muscle. It is both corrective and therapeutic.

Hot stone massage services is a variation on classic massage therapy. Heated stones are moved over key points on the body. The use of hot stones for healing dates back to ancient times.

Chair Massage Services

Chair massage services is performed in the workplace. Your employees will be seated upright on a special massage chair and will remain fully clothed. No oils are used, so there is no need for them to be concerned about stains on clothing. The session generally lasts from ten to twenty minutes. The chair is lightweight and portable, enabling it to be set up in virtually any office or break room.
